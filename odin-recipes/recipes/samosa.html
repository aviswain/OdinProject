Butter Chicken

Description

A pastry with a savory filling, mostly vegetables, spiced potatoes, onions, peas, also non-vegetarian meat, or fish. It is made into different shapes, including triangular, cone, or crescent, depending on the region.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Mix vegetables
  2. Stuff pastry
  3. Fry it
